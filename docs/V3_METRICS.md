# V3 Metrics and Validation

## Product funnel

- Load success.
- Tutorial start/completion.
- First match start/completion.
- Rematch.
- D1 and D7 return.
- Queue wait and cancellation.

## Gameplay

- Deflect tier.
- Rally and round duration.
- Damage and KO.
- Shot selection.
- Map side win.
- Quit timing.

## Technical

- FPS/frame-time buckets.
- Draw calls and triangles.
- Texture/geometries count.
- Ping, packet loss and desync.
- Reconnect and migration outcome.
- Runtime errors and crash-free sessions.

## Economy

- Currency source/sink.
- Item preview.
- Purchase conversion.
- Free unlock time.
- Battle-pass progression.
- Duplicate conversion.

## Initial gates

- Tutorial completion: 70%.
- First-match completion: 80%.
- D1: 20%.
- D7: 8%.
- Crash-free: 99%.
- Typical queue: under 60 seconds.

These are internal starting targets and must be replaced by observed cohort data.
